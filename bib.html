Below is a table of all references from which data for the Open Cataclysmic Variable Catalog is drawn that has a bibcode entry on NASA’s ADS. Some of the references we draw from may not have specified a bibcode, and thus this list may be incomplete, although we make a concerted effort to ensure that bibcodes are included in source information.
